Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Kids Story App?

Kids Story App is an AI-powered storytelling app that creates unique, personalized stories for children. You can read them, listen to narration, and save your favorites.

🧠 How does the story generator work?

Our app uses artificial intelligence to generate original stories based on topics and characters you choose. Each story is unique, creative and designed for kids.

🎧 Can the stories be narrated aloud?

Yes! Stories can be read aloud with high-quality narration. You can choose between male and female voices and control playback easily.

🔖 Can I save my favorite stories?

Yes. Saved stories are stored securely so you can access them later. Your stories sync with your device.

💰 Is the app free?

You can use the app for free with limited. Premium subscriptions unlock unlimited stories and audio playback.

🛒 How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are available weekly, monthly or yearly and auto-renew unless canceled. You can manage them in your device's settings under subscriptions.

👪 Is Kids Story App safe for children?

Yes. The app was designed for kids. We do not collect personal data from children and provide a Parental Gate for purchases or links.

🔐 Do you collect personal data?

No personal data is collected from children. Some data (like saved stories) may be stored securely if you're signed in. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

kids name and age are saved on the user device and we don't collect them

🔄 I updated the app but don’t see changes. What should I do?

Try restarting the app, checking for updates in the App Store or Play Store, or refreshing the screen to see the latest features.

🧾 How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple or Google. You can request one at:

  • Apple Support
  • Google Play Support
